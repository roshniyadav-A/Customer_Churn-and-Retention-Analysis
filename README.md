# 📊 Customer Churn Prediction & Retention Analytics

> **An End-to-End Data Analytics, Business Intelligence & Machine Learning Project**

This project demonstrates a complete analytics workflow to analyze customer behavior, identify key churn drivers, predict customer churn probability, and generate actionable business insights using **PostgreSQL, SQL, Python, Machine Learning, and Power BI**.

---

# 📖 Introduction

Customer retention is one of the most critical business challenges for subscription-based companies. Losing existing customers directly impacts recurring revenue, customer lifetime value, and long-term business growth.

This project simulates a real-world business scenario where customer data is analyzed to understand churn behavior, identify high-risk customers, and provide data-driven recommendations to improve customer retention through Business Intelligence and Predictive Analytics.

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

- Analyze customer demographics and subscription behavior.
- Identify the key drivers influencing customer churn.
- Perform data cleaning and exploratory data analysis (EDA).
- Build predictive Machine Learning models for churn prediction.
- Develop an executive Power BI dashboard for business reporting.
- Generate actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

---

# 💼 Business Problem

Subscription-based businesses often struggle to identify customers who are likely to leave their services. Without understanding customer behavior and churn patterns, organizations may experience increased revenue loss and reduced customer lifetime value.

This project addresses this challenge by analyzing customer data to uncover churn patterns and support proactive retention strategies through data-driven decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Project Purpose

The purpose of this project is to demonstrate an end-to-end Data Analytics solution that follows the complete analytics lifecycle—from data collection and preprocessing to predictive modeling, business intelligence reporting, and strategic recommendations.

It showcases practical skills required for **Data Analyst**, **Business Intelligence Analyst**, and **Product Analyst** roles.
